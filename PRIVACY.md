# Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 15, 2026

Skriuw is a keyboard-first note-taking application for writing, organizing, and syncing notes across devices. This Privacy Policy explains what information Skriuw collects, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

This document is a practical template for the Skriuw project. Before using it for a production service, review it with qualified legal counsel and replace all placeholder contact details.

## Information We Collect

Skriuw may collect the following information when you use the application:

- Account information, such as your email address, authentication provider identity, and profile details needed to sign in.
- User content, including notes, folders, journal entries, tags, and related metadata that you create or edit in the app.
- App preferences and settings, such as editor mode, layout preferences, profile settings, and AI provider configuration.
- AI provider key metadata if you choose to configure your own AI keys. Secret key values should be stored encrypted server-side when encryption is configured.
- Usage and diagnostic data, such as error reports, AI usage logs, request metadata, device information, browser information, and timestamps.
- Payment or billing information only if paid features are added. Payment details should be processed by a dedicated payment provider and not stored directly by Skriuw.

## How We Use Information

Skriuw uses information to:

- Provide, sync, and secure your notes, folders, journal entries, tags, and settings.
- Authenticate users and protect accounts.
- Save preferences and personalize the editor experience.
- Support optional AI features when you choose to use them.
- Monitor reliability, debug errors, prevent abuse, and improve the product.
- Communicate service updates, security notices, or support responses.
- Comply with legal obligations.

## User Content

Your notes and other user-created content remain your content. Skriuw stores and processes that content only to provide the application and related features.

Skriuw does not sell your notes or use them for advertising. If AI features are enabled, selected content may be sent to the configured AI provider only as needed to complete the action you requested.

## AI Features

Skriuw may include optional AI-powered features. When you use those features:

- The text or context needed for the request may be sent to the selected AI provider.
- The provider may process that content according to its own terms and privacy policy.
- Usage data may be logged to help track cost, reliability, and abuse prevention.
- If you configure your own provider keys, you are responsible for making sure you have permission to use those keys.

Do not use AI features with content that you are not allowed to share with the configured provider.

## Authentication and Service Providers

Skriuw uses third-party infrastructure providers to operate the service. These may include:

- Supabase for authentication, database storage, and backend services.
- OAuth providers such as Google or GitHub when you choose to sign in with them.
- Hosting, analytics, observability, email, payment, or AI providers if configured for a deployment.

These providers process information only as needed to provide their services to Skriuw, subject to their own terms and privacy commitments.

## Data Retention

Skriuw retains account information and user content for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide the service.

If account deletion is enabled, deleting your account should remove or anonymize account-linked data unless retention is required for security, backup, fraud prevention, or legal reasons. Backups may retain deleted data for a limited period before automatic removal.

## Security

Skriuw uses technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect user data. These may include row-level access controls, encrypted transport, server-only service credentials, and encryption for sensitive stored values where configured.

No online service can guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for keeping your login credentials secure and for using strong authentication practices.

## Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

- Access the personal information associated with your account.
- Correct inaccurate account or profile information.
- Export or copy your notes.
- Delete your account and associated content.
- Object to or restrict certain processing.

To make a privacy request, contact: `privacy@example.com`.

## International Processing

Skriuw and its service providers may process information in countries other than your country of residence. Those countries may have different data protection laws.

## Children

Skriuw is not intended for children under 13, or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction. Do not use Skriuw if you are below the required age.

## Changes to This Policy

Skriuw may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes should be communicated through the app, by email, or by updating the effective date above.
